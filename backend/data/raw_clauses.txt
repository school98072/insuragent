Homeowners Policy Exclusions - Section B.4 (Wear and Tear / Gradual Deterioration)
The Company shall not be liable for loss or damage caused by:
a) Gradual deterioration, wear and tear, rust, corrosion, rot, mold, or latent defects.
b) Damage arising from maintenance neglect, slow leakage or seepage of water over a period exceeding 14 days, or gradual structural shifting.
c) Accidental burst of pipes is covered, but damages resulting from failure to maintain plumbing fixtures are excluded.

Motor Insurance Policy - Section III (Medical Expenses Reimbursement)
1. The Company will pay the reasonable medical expenses incurred in connection with accidental bodily injury sustained by the Insured or any authorized driver while getting into, riding in, or getting out of the Car.
2. The maximum limit of indemnity for any single event under this Section shall not exceed $6,000 HKD.
3. Medical claims must be accompanied by original official medical receipts, physician's diagnosis, and police collision reports.

Credit Card Comprehensive Travel Insurance - Section D.2 (Flight Delay Benefit)
1. If the departure of the scheduled public conveyance in which the Insured Person is traveling is delayed at the direction of the carrier for at least 6 consecutive hours from the scheduled time due to inclement weather, strike, industrial action, or mechanical breakdown.
2. The Company will pay $1,000 HKD for the first 6 hours of delay, and $500 HKD for each subsequent 6 hours, up to a maximum limit of $5,000 HKD.
3. This benefit is payable only if the travel tickets were purchased using the eligible Credit Card prior to departure.
4. Exclusions: Delays due to carrier cancellation where alternative transportation is offered within 4 hours, or delays due to the Insured Person's failure to check in on time.
